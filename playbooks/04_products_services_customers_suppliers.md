# Playbook 04: Products, Services, Architecture, Customers, Suppliers, Moat, and AI Capabilities

Agents: product_services_agent, architecture_agent, product_moat_agent, customer_supplier_agent, ai_ml_genai_agent. Identify all major products, platforms, modules, SKUs, services, technical capabilities, deployment models, tenant models, architecture signals, data architecture, integrations, APIs, standards, security, resiliency, AI/automation, ecosystem dependencies, product maturity, limitations, customer proof, and product-specific moat.

Required tables: Product Catalog, Product Technical Capability Matrix, Product Architecture Matrix, Product Architecture Risk Matrix, Product Moat Matrix, AI/ML/GenAI Capability Matrix, Customer and Supplier Mapping.

## Output Format

Return Markdown only. Do not produce HTML, CSS, JavaScript, final files, or standalone JSON.

## Minimum Depth

In exhaustive mode, this playbook must contribute at least 500 words of narrative analysis excluding tables unless public evidence is unavailable. If evidence is unavailable, explain what was searched, what was not found, and what due-diligence questions remain.

## Guardrails

Do not invent facts. Include raw URLs while drafting; the orchestrator will convert them to numbered references. Use `Not found in public sources reviewed` when evidence is unavailable.
